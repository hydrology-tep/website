Hydrological Modelling

Developed by:

The Hydrological Modelling service allows users to access the following data:

  • River discharge
  • Lake outflow
  • Land surface water balance components such as precipitation, evapotranspiration and soil moisture

This thematic app provides data from simulation of historical periods as well as short-term and seasonal forecasts.

The geographical area covered by the Hydrological Modelling thematic app is the domain of the Niger HYPE model that covers the Niger river basin.

In order to get started with the Hydrological Modelling service, you can read the documentation and the service pricing.
